Admission Process

We welcome students from diverse backgrounds to join our inclusive learning environment. Applications open every Baisakh for the upcoming academic year.

Steps to Apply

  1. Fill out the online application form.
  2. Attend an orientation session with parents.
  3. Submit necessary documents and previous academic records.
  4. Receive confirmation and join the Bright Future family!
